Recycling

The enviromental effects of recycling

Recycling has the poitive effect on the enviroment of turning waste materials such as plastic into somthing more useful as opposed to just burying it underground which in theory reduces the amount of pollution on the planet considerably however though the reality is that most materials cannot actually be recycled at all or very effeciantly and as such most materials and alot of plastic just ends up being buried in a landfill site instead of being recycled further adding to the already large amount of pollution on the Earth.

What you can do to help

You can help to reduce the negative effects that recycling has on the enviroment by simply buying consumable items that are contained within metal or glass containers both of which are theoreticaly infinitly recycalabe as opposed to plastic which often cannot be recycled at all and takes many years to fully biodegrade.
